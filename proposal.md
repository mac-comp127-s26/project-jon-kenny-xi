## Members

- Jonathan
- Kenny
- Xi

## Description

Our idea for this project is to make a Star Wars version of the Angry Birds game. The Angry Birds would be dressed as Star Wars characters and the pigs would be dressed as Stormtroopers or other villains in Star Wars. The different types of birds (Red, Yellow, Black, Blue, etc.) would have different powers/abilities as they are launched towards the pigs. Our project is cool/interesting because it's a different take on the Angry Birds game with a cooler design, and I think it will be a game people will want to play. 

## Initial Viable Product

Our initial version of the project would be just one level of an Angry Birds game without the designs or even the powers, just getting the birds to fly into the buildings and pigs to win. We could look at the previous Bubble homework to take some inspiration on how to initially start coding for our project.
